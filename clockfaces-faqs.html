Clock Faces Help

How do I change my clock face to 24/12 hour time?

Changing your watch to 24/12 hour time actually has nothing to do with the clock face. Go to fitbit.com. Click on the gear icon. Go to Settings > Personal Info > Advanced Settings > Clock Display Time and switch to the desired units.
